# Dangerous issue policy

A dangerous issue is one that could create a safety risk if it escaped final delivery readiness review.
Examples include brake-line or brake-fluid issues, exposed electrical conductors near the battery, and loose critical fasteners.
Cosmetic marks, cleaning defects, and minor panel finish issues are not dangerous by themselves.
The live dataset stores the official Y/N flag; free-text notes may provide supporting context but should not override the flag.
